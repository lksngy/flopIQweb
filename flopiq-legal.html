01 — Overview

Our Approach to Privacy

FlopIQ is built to require as little personal information as possible. You do not need to create an account, provide an email address, or share any personal details to use the App.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with advertisers. We collect only what is necessary to make the App work.

02 — What We Collect

Data Collected

The following information is collected when you use FlopIQ:

  • Anonymous User ID — automatically generated by Firebase when you install the App. This is a random identifier with no connection to your identity.
  • Learning Progress — lessons completed, exercise scores, accuracy stats, and iteration progress. Stored to power your personal training plan.
  • Purchase History — whether you have an active subscription, managed via RevenueCat. No payment details are stored by us.
  • App Usage Data — screen views and interaction events collected via Firebase Analytics to help us understand how the App is used and improve it.
  • Crash Reports — anonymous crash and error data via Firebase Crashlytics to help us fix bugs.

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, or any other personal identifying information.

03 — How We Use It

Purpose of Data Collection

All data collected is used exclusively for:

  • Saving and syncing your learning progress across sessions
  • Verifying your subscription status to unlock premium content
  • Improving the App based on aggregate usage patterns
  • Identifying and fixing crashes and errors

Data is never used for advertising, sold to third parties, or shared with any party for marketing purposes.

04 — Third-Party Services

Services We Use

FlopIQ relies on the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics) — data processing by Google LLC. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription and purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — payment processing and app distribution. Apple Privacy Policy
05 — Data Storage

Where Your Data Lives

Your progress and anonymous ID are stored in Google Firebase servers. Firebase operates data centers globally; your data may be stored and processed in the United States or other countries where Google operates infrastructure.

Some data is also stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework, allowing the App to function without a constant internet connection.

  • GDPR / CCPA — if you are a resident of the EU or California, you have additional rights including the right to access, correct, and delete your personal data. Contact us to exercise these rights.
